WEBSITE

Creating a new frontend application and a unique brand identity for RiceApps

Problem

The current application is not easily updatable, and populated with irrelevant information. The overall design can be bettered to reflect RiceApps.

It is not enough.

We need to create a forum where all information relevant to RiceApps is easily available and amenable. This information consists of:

1. Ongoing projects with relevant sources

2. Forum for discussion and suggestions

3. Information on club's student body

4. Other CS related opportunities, activities, or organizations

Users

Our users involve a broad spectrum of students and employers.

1. Prospective students looking to gain information about RiceApps

2. Current students interested in ongoing CS related projects/events/opportunities

3. Employers interested in RiceApps projects
